🧭 Brand Identity

Dos Bold Latinas Podcast

A brand identity project celebrating Latina voices through bold typography, warm color choices, and flexible visual application.

📜 Expedition Brief

The Dos Bold Latinas Podcast brand identity was created to celebrate the strength, resilience, and authenticity of Latina women. The goal of this expedition was to develop a visual identity that felt bold and empowering while remaining approachable and versatile across multiple platforms. The design process focused on creating a memorable logo system supported by a warm color palette, thoughtful typography choices, and practical brand applications. From digital media to merchandise, every element was designed to communicate confidence, community, and cultural pride while providing the flexibility needed for a growing podcast brand.

The Mission

Design a cohesive visual identity that empowers the Dos Bold Latinas Podcast by celebrating Latina voices through bold typography, purposeful color, and a flexible brand system that can be applied consistently across digital platforms, merchandise, and future marketing materials.

🌱 Lessons from the Trail

Brand Systems Matter

This project reinforced the importance of designing a complete brand system rather than a standalone logo. Through the development of Dos Bold Latinas, I learned how color, typography, and visual consistency work together to strengthen a brand's message and create a cohesive experience.

Context Brings Designs to Life

The expedition also highlighted the value of presentation and context. Seeing the identity applied to merchandise and real-world mockups transformed the design from a collection of visual elements into a living brand.

Design Starts with the Audience

Most importantly, I learned that successful branding begins with understanding the audience and creating visuals that authentically reflect their story, values, and aspirations.
