About Me

"Building thoughtful experiences where education, design, and technology come together to make learning more meaningful."

Who I Am

I approach every project with a balance of creativity and precision. Whether I'm building a website, developing a brand identity, designing educational resources, or writing code, I enjoy solving problems through thoughtful design and continuous learning. My background as both an educator and a designer allows me to think beyond aesthetics and focus on creating experiences that are intuitive, engaging, and accessible.

My mission is to help bridge the gap between traditional education and technology. I believe the future of learning is not about replacing great teaching. It is about enhancing it through thoughtful design, purposeful technology, and meaningful human connection. I hope to create tools and experiences that empower both educators and learners to grow with confidence.

Teach. Design. Create

These three words have become more than a personal motto. They represent the instersection of my passsions and foundations of everything I build.

📚 Teach

As an educator, I believe learning should be engaging, meningful, and accessible. My experience in the classroom has taught me that great instruction begins with empathy, thoughtful design, and deep understanding of how people learn.

🎨 Design

Design allows me to transform ideas into meaningful visual experiences. Whether developing a brand identity, creating marketing materials, or designing user interfaces, I enjoy finding creative solutions that communicate clearly while remaining visually engaging.

💻 Create

Development brings those ideas to life. Through HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and continuous learning, I enjoy building responsive websites and interactive experiences that combine creativity with functionality. Every project is another opportunity to learn, experiment, and improve.

My Journey

My path into web development has been anything but traditional. I earned a Bachelor of Arts in History before discovering a passion for design and completing an Associate of Applied Arts and Sciences in Graphic Design and Web Development. Along the way, I built a career in education, where I learned that the most meaningful experiences happen when creativity, communication, and problem solving come together.

Today, those experiences influence every project I create. My background as a historian taught me to research and tell compelling stories. Teaching strengthened my ability to communicate complex ideas with clarity and empathy. Graphic design sharpened my visual thinking, while web development allows me to build interactive experiences that bring those ideas to life.

Rather than seeing these disciplines as separate careers, I view them as complementary skills that continue to shape the way I think, design, and create.

Looking Ahead

I believe technology should enhance human connection, not replace it. My current focus is strengthening my front-end development skills while continuing to build projects through my Developer Hub that combine thoughtful design, clean code, and meaningful storytelling.

Long term, I hope to help shape the future of education by creating digital experiences that bridge traditional teaching with modern technology. Whether designing websites, developing educational resources, or building interactive learning tools, my goal remains the same: create work that informs, inspires, and leaves a lasting impact.

Teach. Design. Create. Those three words continue to guide every expedition I take.
